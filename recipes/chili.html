Chili

Ingredients

  • Dry Rancho Gordo Black Beans
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Olive Oil
  • Bacon Bits
  • Beef chunks
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Cumin Powder
  • Bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Soak the Racho Gordo black beans for at least 6 hours
  2. Cook the beans in their soaking water with olive oil, onion, celery, and carrots for four hours at a simmer
  3. Take the beans and liquid out of the pot and put to the side
  4. Color the bacon bits and beef chunks to release the flavors in the pot
  5. Pour the beans and liquid back into the pot
  6. Empty the tomato sauce into the pot
  7. Add the seasonings to the pot and let it cook for on a low simmer for 1.5 hours
  8. Enjoy!
