Ice Cream Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Two Boxes of Pilsbury Sugar Cookies
  • Tub Vanilla Ice Cream

Instructions

  1. Follow the instructions on the Pilsbury sugar cookie box except only cook it for 8 minutes
  2. Set the sugar cookies aside to cool
  3. Line a casserole dish with wax paper then place the cooled cookies inside
  4. Spread the vanilla ice cream on the layer of the cookies then put the second layer of cookies on top
  5. Put the cookies in the fridge for at least 4 hours to refreeze the ice cream
